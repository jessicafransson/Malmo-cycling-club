Ride with us

We find cycling with friends to be very enjoyable, therefor we have 3 days a week where we go cycling together. Join us on tuesdays for a mutual Road ride, the distance varies - but its always a social event. On thursdays we go for the gravels together, one week focus technique and the next distance. On saturdays we do a long ride on our road bikes, always with a coffee stop along the way.
