Most things begins and ends with coffee, so even cycling

Wether you love cycling or not, you'll find your place in our café, we deliver nice espresso from our Faema E61 and make lovely lattes.
If you prefer pure brew or a cold drink, we have that as well,
We have only the best beans from a local roastery here in Malmö to serve you fresh coffee with locally produced milk. We also have a small selection of pastries from the bakery down the road.
Welcome in, wether you're sweaty or nice and tidy, we would love to see you!
