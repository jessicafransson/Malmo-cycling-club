About us

We're a local café for cyclists, or just coffee lovers - pop by in your finest cycling gear or your everyday wear!

We have only the best beans from a local roastery here in Malmö to serve you fresh coffee with locally produced milk. We also have a small selection of pastries from the bakery down the road.
