Malmö Cycling club

Malmö, Skåne County, Sweden

This is what we do:

Roadcycling

Let's explore the magic of Skåne country roads together. Every tuesday we do a ride together, starting and finishing the ride together.

Gravel rides

Every saturday we go exploring the forrests and have a break in one of the lovely cafés around Skåne.

Mountainbike

We also arrange Mountainbike rides, on a track close to Malmö or go for specific routes outside the city.

Race-pace

Every friday morning its all on his/her own when we do full speed and finish with a nice brekafast together
