Welcome to our cycling club

From us you start your bike ride after a nice coffee, or join us to watch the Tour over a cappucino.
We run this cycling café with the intention of gathering Malmös cyclists, with the same passion for good coffee as us.
Let us help you with some new inspirational routes, or just give you some energy for your ride with a freshly baked pastry!
